How To Use The PIS

In order to effectively and appropriately use this PIS system, all personnel are required to read the following steps carefully. Only after reading and understanding how to fill the PIS form, should the form be filled by personnel. Please note that changes cannot be made to the data when submitted, except by the Administrators. The steps are listed below as:

Step 1

All personnel should have only valid and up-to-date data on the following:

  • First Name
  • Middle Name
  • Last Name
  • Age
  • Phone Number
  • State Of Origin
  • Email Address
  • Residential Address
  • Gender
  • Department
  • Staff Type
  • Position Held
  • Highest Certification Acquired


Step 2

After having the valid and up-to-date information of the items stated above are personnel required to proceed to the next step.


Step 3

Personnel should ensure to cross-check the form completely and more than once after its filled to avoid mistakes or incorrect data.


Step 4

If a personnel encounters an issue or problems while filling the form, said personnel can contact our Administrators by clicking here and describing the issue to the Admin.


Step 5

If a personnel makes a mistake, inputs incorrect or not up-to-date data while filling the form, said personnel can contact our Administrators to modify and make neccessary changes to the personnel information. Personnel can click here to contact the Admin.


Step 6

After reading and understanding the above steps, personnel can fill the form by clicking on the button below.
