About The PIS

Personnel Information System(PIS)

A Personnel Information System (PIS) can be best explained and defined as a computerized system or computer-based system or application whose purpose is conservation, upkeep, and maintenance of the records, files, and registers of individuals in an organization. This Personnel Information System was designed in line of the points stated earlier.

Asides the above points, The Personnel Information System offers the following extra services listed in the Services Page

Services

Montoring & Logging

The PIS effectively logs, monitoring and records all personnel data being inserted into the system.

User Friendly

The PIS system is easy to use and understand to all personnel that want to insert their data/information.

Accuracy & Speed

The PIS system offers fast and accurate storage of personnel data/information.

Effective Code & Database

The PIS provides a seamless code and efficient database.

No Data Loss

The online PIS system doesnt lose data unless deleted by the admininstrators unlike the manual pis system.

Administative Support

If personnel encounter an issue, you can contact the admin for assistance.

PIS Form

In order to effectively and appropriately use this PIS system, all personnel are required to read the following steps carefully. Only after reading and understanding how to fill the PIS form, should the form be filled by personnel. Please note that changes cannot be made to the data when submitted, except by the Administrators. The steps are listed below.


Step 1
All personnel should have only valid and up-to-date data on the following:
  • First Name
  • Middle Name
  • Last Name
  • Age
  • Phone Number
  • State Of Origin
  • Email Address
  • Residential Address
  • Gender
  • Department
  • Staff Type
  • Position Held
  • Highest Certification Acquired



Step 2
After having the valid and up-to-date information of the items stated above are personnel required to proceed to the next step.



Step 3
Personnel should ensure to cross-check the form completely and more than once after its filled to avoid mistakes or incorrect data.

Step 4
If a personnel encounters an issue or problems while filling the form, said personnel can contact our Administratorsand describing the issue to the Admin.



Step 5
If a personnel makes a mistake, inputs incorrect or not up-to-date data while filling the form, said personnel can contact our Administrators to modify and make neccessary changes to the personnel information. Personnel can click here to contact the Admin.



Step 6
After reading and understanding the above steps, personnel can fill the form by clicking here



Step 7
To view the details entered by personnel(you), click here. Your credentials are your email address and your password is your Middle name.
